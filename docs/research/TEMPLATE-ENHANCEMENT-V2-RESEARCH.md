## üîë Critical Discoveries

> **Minto's Pyramid Approach**: Start with takeaways, end with details.

### Discovery 1: CRAAP Test for Source Evaluation

**TL;DR**: Use Currency, Relevance, Authority, Accuracy, Purpose to evaluate sources systematically.

**Source**: Multiple academic and technical writing sources (Published: 2024-2025)
**Impact**: üî¥ High
**Confidence**: High (verified by 3+ sources)

**Summary**:
The CRAAP test provides a standardized framework for evaluating source quality. Each criterion has a weighted contribution to overall reliability assessment.

**Application**:
Added Source Evaluation Criteria table to research template with weighted scoring.

### Discovery 2: Di√°taxis Framework for Documentation

**TL;DR**: Organize documentation into four types: Tutorials, How-To Guides, Reference, Explanation.

**Source**: [GitBook Documentation Structure](https://gitbook.com/docs/guides/docs-best-practices/documentation-structure-tips) (Published: 2025)
**Impact**: üî¥ High
**Confidence**: High

**Summary**:
The Di√°taxis framework (created by Daniele Procida) categorizes documentation by user needs, leading to better organization and discoverability.

**Application**:
Added Guide Type field to guide template, organized sections by Di√°taxis categories.

### Discovery 3: Validation Sections are Essential

**TL;DR**: All insights and research should include explicit validation methods and success metrics.

**Source**: Product discovery and UX research best practices (Published: 2024-2025)
**Impact**: üî¥ High
**Confidence**: High

**Summary**:
Without validation sections, insights remain unverified assumptions. Templates should explicitly prompt for verification methods.

**Application**:
Added Validation section with success metrics tables to insight and research templates.

### Discovery 4: Quick Links Improve Navigation

**TL;DR**: Every document over 100 lines should have a Quick Links section at the top.

**Source**: Knowledge base and technical writing best practices (Published: 2025)
**Impact**: üü° Medium
**Confidence**: High

**Summary**:
Users scan documents first, then read. Quick links enable immediate access to relevant sections.

**Application**:
Added Quick Links sections to all four templates.

### Discovery 5: Confidence Calibration Matters

**TL;DR**: Explicitly rating confidence prevents overreliance on uncertain information.

**Source**: UX research and product discovery methodologies (Published: 2024-2025)
**Impact**: üü° Medium
**Confidence**: High

**Summary**:
Without explicit confidence ratings, all information appears equally reliable. Star ratings and multi-factor assessments improve decision quality.

**Application**:
Added Confidence Assessment tables to research and insight templates.
